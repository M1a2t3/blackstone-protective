About Blackstone Protective

Blackstone Protective is a leading security solutions provider based in Nigeria, specializing in comprehensive, reliable, and innovative protective services.

Blackstone Protective is a leading security solutions provider based in Nigeria. We specialize in delivering comprehensive, reliable, and innovative protective services to individuals, businesses, and organizations across the country. At Blackstone Protective, we understand that security is not just about protection it is about peace of mind. Founded with a vision to set new standards in the Nigerian security industry, we combine advanced technology, highly trained personnel, and strategic intelligence to safeguard what matters most to our clients.

Who We Are

We are a team of seasoned security professionals with extensive experience in both private and public sector protection. Our operatives are rigorously trained, vetted, and equipped to handle a wide range of security challenges in Lagos and beyond. From executive protection and corporate security to residential guarding, event security, and risk assessment, we provide tailored solutions that meet the unique needs of each client. Our name, Blackstone, draws inspiration from strength, resilience, and unyielding protection qualities we embody in every service we deliver.
